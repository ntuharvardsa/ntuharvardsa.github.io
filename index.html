Frank

Frank (Chih-Yuan) Chiu is a current third-year NTU student double majoring in electrical engineering and physics.

His unique experience as a former American high school student and a current Taiwanese undergraduate student gives him the rare ability to empathize with both Asian and American students. Cognition varies from individual to individual because everyone comes from a distinct cultural, socioeconomic, and political background.

Years of engaging in cultural and academic exchange in the United States has taught him that only through nuanced dialogue and cultural exchange can the global community of students collectively paint a more encompassing and descriptive portrait of education, and of life itself.

As a student who has years of experience conducting such dialogue with friends and teachers around the globe, Frank is honored to have the opportunity to engage in cultural and academic exchange at HCAP conferences.

He can be contacted through his NTU email b03901169@ntu.edu.tw or through his Gmail, chiuap5@gmail.com.
