ABOUT

2017 is the first year that NTU has had a chance to partner with Harvard and participate in the Harvard College in Asia Program, which we regard as a bridge between Harvard and NTU.

As students in Taiwan participate events through the conference, we hope that they will have a global mindset and raise awareness of global issues.

Meanwhile, Harvard students will be exposed to the environment of Taiwan and have a deeper understanding of the difficulties we are facing.

The Taipei Conference connects us to the world and lights up Taiwan on the globe.
